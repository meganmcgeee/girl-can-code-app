Organizations

Here are just a few organizations that are looking to add more women to the tech sector through free to low cost education.

Name Description Cost Location(s)
Girl, Develop It! A non-profit skills building initiative. Free New York
Code Ed A non-profit that partners with school and teaches computer science to girls in underserved communities, starting in middle school. Free New York City

Iridescent an education non-profit that trains professional engineers, scientists, and parents to deliver cutting-edge STEM education to underserved girls, children and their families Free National
Geek Girl Geek Girl empowers women & girls of all ages in technology, from novice beginner to startup savant. We do this through full day Tech Conferences & workshops. Free National
Women Techmakers Women Techmakers is a Google-run service for aspiring or current female techonologists that offers curated resources, exclusive events, and a network of other female technologists. Free National
Mother Coders a tech orientation program that provides on-site childcare for mothers who want to learn basic coding, gain a deeper understanding of the technology landscape, and network with peers and industry professionals. Free National
The New York Fashion Tech Lab The New York Fashion Tech Lab is a community-driven, relationship building, collaboration & business development platform. Free National
Dev Chix A diverse group of women from a variety of technical and experience background, with a common goal of providing education, support, and mentorship to current and future women in technology Free International
Made With Code A diverse group of women from a variety of technical and experience background, with a common goal of providing education, support, and mentorship to current and future women in technology Free International
She's Coding She’s Coding is an open-source project developed in cooperation with the documentary film CODE: Debugging the Gender Gap. She’s Coding provides education, resources, and actionable guidelines for anyone who wants to help bridge the gender gap in the field of computer science Free International
Stemettes Stemettes seeks to To inspire the next generation of females into Science, Technology, Engineering and Maths (STEM) fields by showing them the amazing women already in STEM via a series of panel events, hackathons, exhibitions, and mentoring schemes. Free International
Outbox Incubator Outbox Incubator is an initiative by Stemettes to provide seed funding, intensive mentorship, and support to talented girls aged 22 and under who have innovative business and technology ideas Free International
Techbridge Girls TBG seeks to increase the number of women and underrepresented minorities in science, technology, and engineering via after-school programs. Three are in place at schools spanning three Bay Area school districts. They also provide training to school districts and partners to build capacity for STEM teaching in after-school and summer programs. Free Bay Area
